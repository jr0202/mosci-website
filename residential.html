Our 4 Step Process

Modern Structures & Civil Engineering (MOSCI) offers the best in construction services,
both ground up work and remodeling, below is some information on how to get the job started.

1. Connect
Setting up an appointment is easy! It's time to get all of your questions answered. Starting your project only takes a minute.

Contact us by email or by phone (+263 71 524 5626). We are also always available on WhatsApp on the same number.
2. Consult
Describe your requirements!

Meet with us and we will discuss what you need for your home, then we will collect any existing information, or documents you may have.

We will then establish a target budget with you, having an idea of what you budget is will allow us to recommend options for your house.
3. Create
  • Floor plans and elevations that are construction ready..
  • Fill in a client work sheet. Information from the worksheet will be used in determining the cost estimate. If you have an interior designer this is the time to get them involved, if they aren’t already.
  • Structural details including footing layout, beam and column sizing, framing plans, roof structural plan, septic design and site plan.
  • Electrical plan including lighting layout and switch/receptacle layout.
  • Interior elevations of cabinetry and other details as needed.
  • A comprehensive price proposal. At MOSCI our proposals are in-depth and thorough.
4. Complete
See your project completed quickly and efficiently. Make your payment and it's all done!

Modern Structures & Civil Engineering (MOSCI) was approved for inclusion on the Registered List of Bidders for the category of construction and civil Works (buildings, dams, roads etc), in terms of Section 4 of the Public Procurement and Disposal of Public Assets (General) by the Procurement Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe. MOSCI is a highly dependable and trustworthy construction company that has proved its worth in the Zimbabwean construction industry.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below we compiled a list of questions that are usually asked by MOSCI's customers, with the answers,
yours may be answered too. Get in touch for more enquiries.

  • What is MOSCI’s service area?

    The greater part of Zimbabwe and we are soon to expand into the whole of Southern Africa. Call us to find out more.

  • What type of work does MOSCI do?

    All sorts of construction work including new builds and remodels. Commercial construction, residential contruction or even repairs, we do it all at MOSCI.

  • Is MOSCI registered and certified?

    MOSCI is a registered construction company with all certifications relevant to the business.

  • Can I supply my own materials?

    Yes you can suppy your own materials, but there is greater advantage in letting MOSCI receive, check, store, deliver and generally be responsible for the materials used in your project.

  • Can I use my own Subcontractors?

    Yes you can.

  • How long does it take to complete a job?

    Discuss your proposal process, start date, and build time at our first meeting. All jobs are unique and some steps of the process rely on your timeliness. We may be able to start in days, weeks, or months. Your job may be completed in days, weeks, or months. Knowing your timeline needs at the beginning of the process will help us deliver on time.

  • How close is your price estimation to the actual cost?

    After we consider everything you will make all of your selections and receive your fixed price before you sign your contract.

  • Do I need an architect?

    MOSCI Engineering can design your project in-house allowing for the most accurate construction pricing. Letting MOSCI do the design ensures your build starts with practical knowledge of feasibility, materials used, costs, and will save you money. MOSCI will even provide 3D computer renderings of your project so you can know what it will look like before we start working. We can help advise you on your best steps to proceed.

  • Do I need an interior designer?

    The selections process can be done with or without an interior designer. If you know what you like or have a style in mind you can make all of the selections with the help of our selections coordinator. Get an interior designer to make cohesive selections that will not only match your style, but ties together your furniture with your fixtures, your color with your textures, and your finished with your architecture.

  • Do I need to move out of my house during the project?

    It depends! We have been flexible on a case by case basis. You can use other rooms while we work on others. The key is proper planning to reduce downtime in your home, keep added expenses down, and to ensure your family’s safety.
